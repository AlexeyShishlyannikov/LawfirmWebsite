Who We Are

The principal attorney and founder of Kesselman Law Firm, Roman Kesselman, has been practicing law since 2004. He graduated from the University of Washington in Seattle, WA, with a Bachelor Degree in Business Administration in 1996 and went on to get his Degree in Jurisprudence from the Willamette College of Law in Salem, OR, graduating in 2004. He has represented clients in State and Federal District Courts in the State of Washington, Immigration Courts in various states, the Board of Immigration Appeals and The 9th Circuit Federal Court of Appeals.
